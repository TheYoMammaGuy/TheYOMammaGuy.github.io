Gaming In VR

Gaming in VR is one of the most well known uses of VR and is the one that is most readily avalable. Gaming in VR is a very different experience to gaming on a console or PC. Gaming in VR is what a lot of popular VR headsets are made for with some of them connecting directly to a PC to process the game you want to play. Gaming in VR works by putting you in a 3-D rendered enviornment that you are able to interact with by the use of controllers in each hand that use motion tracking. The headset itself is able to know its own location in the game by the use of on-board cameras or an external cameras or sensors that use infared light LEDs that are not visible to the human eye. It can tell how fast your moving and what angle you are at by the use of accelerometers and gyroscopes. The most popular games that are played in VR are Beat Saber, Boneworks, The Elder Scrolls V Skyrin VR and Superhot VR.
