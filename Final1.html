VR In The Medical Feild

Virtual reality also known as VR is an emerginge technology that has a very high growing market. When most people think of VR they think of video games or some sort of entertainment, but virtual reality has many other uses. VR aplication has been introduced to the medical feild for some time it can be used to help surgeons with surgeries that would have previously been very difficult.

VR In The Military

VR can also be used in Rehabilitation to improve the use of upper body motor skills. It is also used in military training, the US and UK have both used Vr to train soldiers into a wide range of simulations. It is esspecialy useful to teach soldiers how to handle a high stress enviornment.

Virtual Reality Market

The Vr market in 2020 was at 15.8 billion dollars and has grown over 6 billion dollars this year and it now at 21.83 billion. It is expected to reach 70 billion by 2028. With the high market over 116 million units are estimated to be produced by 2026.
